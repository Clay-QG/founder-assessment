# Quiet Ground Brand System

## Brand Personality

Quiet Ground should feel calm, intelligent, and grounded.

The tone should communicate clarity, thoughtful analysis, and
operational insight. Avoid flashy or overly technical visual language.

Design should emphasize whitespace, readability, and simplicity.

Keywords: - Calm - Grounded - Intelligent - Minimal - Professional
advisory tone

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Primary Colors

Slate Blue\
#3E5C76

Soft Earth\
#E6E2DD

Deep Charcoal\
#2F2F2F

White\
#FFFFFF

Accent (Subtle tech hint)\
#5A7C8A

Use color sparingly. Quiet Ground design should rely primarily on
whitespace.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Typography

Primary Font\
Inter

Headline Weight\
600

Body Weight\
400

Recommended hierarchy:

H1 -- Inter 600\
H2 -- Inter 600\
Body -- Inter 400

Line height should feel open and readable.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Layout Style

Centered layout

Maximum content width:\
720px

Spacing should be generous.

Use simple card layouts and soft rounded elements.

Avoid heavy borders and excessive UI decoration.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Buttons

Primary Button - Background: Slate Blue (#3E5C76) - Text: White - Border
radius: subtle rounding

Secondary Button - Background: White - Border: Slate Blue - Text: Slate
Blue

Hover states should be subtle.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Graphic Elements

Quiet Ground uses the domino concept as a visual metaphor.

Domino graphics represent: - Operational clarity - Cause and effect in
systems - Information flowing through an organization

Domino graphics should be used sparingly near: - Page headers -
Diagnostic results - Insight sections

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Logo

Quiet Ground logo should appear at the top of the diagnostic interface.

Use a simple placement aligned left or centered depending on layout.

Logo files should be stored in:

/public/qg-logo.svg

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Image Assets

Store brand assets in the public folder:

/public/qg-logo.svg\
/public/domino-graphic.svg

These can be referenced in the application with:

`<img src="/qg-logo.svg" />`{=html}

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Design Philosophy

Quiet Ground products should feel more like a professional advisory tool
than a consumer app.

The experience should feel:

Thoughtful\
Clear\
Analytical\
Trustworthy
